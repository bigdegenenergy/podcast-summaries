  ### Comprehensive Summary


  Sandro Venturini addresses the foundational inefficiency in launching cross-border
  funds: reliance on siloed data and disconnected spreadsheets across legal, distribution,

  legal errors, and reactive management, eroding investor trust and increasing regulatory
  exposure.


  Venturini explains that the initial fund structuring process involves iteratively
  building a **term sheet** by gathering inputs from numerous stakeholders regarding
  investment policy, liquidity terms (redemption frequency, lock-ups), and fee structures.

  The core value proposition of AI lies in its ability to **anticipate potential conflicts**

  concerns of all stakeholders and generate a highly robust, conflict-minimized term

  prospectus required for regulatory filing.

  credit lines or impacting performance definitions.
