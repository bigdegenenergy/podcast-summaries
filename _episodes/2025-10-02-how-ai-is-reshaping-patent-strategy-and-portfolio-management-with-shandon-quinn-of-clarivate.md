  movements and informing product redesigns proactively.

  *   **Competitive Advantage through Benchmarking:** By benchmarking their portfolios

  *   **Build vs. Buy Decision:** A critical current challenge for IP leaders is deciding

  AI will automate the "housekeeping" and manual review tasks, allowing IP professionals
  to focus on high-value strategic analysis, leading to a scenario where R&D teams
  actively engage with patent data because it is interactive and insightful rather
  than cumbersome.
